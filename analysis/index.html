Implementation Checks.

  • In matrix case, homoscedastic tflash gives same results as flash. Looks fine in tensor case.
  • In matriix case, greedy algorithm of tflash same as in flash. Looks fine in tensor case.
  • Backfitting tflash gives reasonable results.
  • Kronecker structured variance gives reasonable estimates, indicating implementation is correct.
  • flash runs about twice as fast as tflash.
  • Justification for using first singular vectors as starting values. Even when starting in random directions, posterior estimates only ever end up at near the first singular vectors or all shrunk to zero.
  • The posterior mean estimates and the empirical singular vectors are super close to each other.
  • The known factor case is coded up correctly.
